FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions:

Do you offer services to write text for my website? +

Yes, we can provide professional content writing services for your website. We ensure the text aligns with your brand voice and is optimized for SEO to attract more visitors. Simply let us know your requirements during the consultation.

Do you work on pre-existing websites? +

Absolutely! We can improve or redesign your existing website to enhance its performance, functionality, and design. Whether it’s updating the look or fixing technical issues, we’ve got you covered.

How do I get a price for my website? +

Our pricing depends on the complexity and features of the website you require. Contact us for a free consultation, where we’ll discuss your needs and provide a tailored quote.

Do you offer custom animations or interactive features? +

Absolutely! We can design and implement custom animations, scroll effects, or interactive elements that make your website more engaging and visually appealing.

Can you create multilingual websites? +

Yes, we can design websites that support multiple languages to help you reach a broader audience. Let us know your language preferences, and we’ll make it happen.

What happens if my website needs updates or changes after it’s live? +

We offer maintenance plans to handle any issues that happen more than 14 days after launching, you can choose either pay as you go or a monthly subscription depending on your needs.

Do you offer training on how to manage my website? +

Absolutely! Once your website is live, we can provide personalized training sessions to help you understand how to manage and update your site, whether it’s adding content, uploading images, or managing e-commerce features.

Can you help migrate my existing website to a new platform? +

Yes, we can assist with seamless website migration, ensuring your content, design, and functionality are retained or improved on the new platform without any downtime or data loss.

Will my website be mobile-friendly and responsive? +

Yes, every website we create is fully responsive and optimized to perform beautifully across all devices, including phones, tablets, and desktops.

Can you integrate advanced features like booking systems or payment gateways? +

Yes, we can integrate advanced functionalities such as appointment booking systems, secure payment gateways, customer management tools, and more to streamline your business operations.

What is your process for understanding my business needs? +

We start with a detailed consultation to understand your goals, target audience, and brand identity. From there, we create a tailored design plan to ensure your website meets your business objectives effectively.

Do you provide hosting and domain registration services? +

Yes, we can handle domain registration and recommend or provide reliable hosting services tailored to your website's needs, ensuring fast loading speeds and uptime.
