Our Mission

In today’s competitive digital landscape, we are committed to crafting exceptional websites that combine outstanding UX/UI design with effective SEO strategies to help businesses grow, rank higher, and reach more customers. From trade and e-commerce to static blog sites, our solutions are tailored to meet the unique needs of every client. Looking ahead, we plan to expand into 3D design and advanced backend development, offering even more features and accessibility. Our mission is to empower smaller businesses to thrive in a world dominated by large corporations, ensuring they gain their fair share through our unwavering dedication and support.

Our Vision

At Web Revive Studio, we aspire to be the trusted provider of website design services, delivering fast, high-ranking websites that help businesses attract new customers. By utilizing custom code and implementing strong SEO practices, we maintain a level of quality and flexibility far beyond that of web builders. Our vision is to uphold the highest standards while simplifying the lives of business owners, giving them back valuable time as we handle the complexities of their online presence. Through dedication and innovation, we strive to be synonymous with excellence and a reliable partner in their growth and success. We want to see you grow!

Our Founder

Lucy Hurley-Wareing

CEO, Founder, and Business Manager

With years of front-end coding experience and a passion for creating visually stunning websites, Lucy founded Web Revive Studio to elevate the standards of web design. Known for meticulous attention to detail and a talent for crafting excellent UX/UI experiences, she ensures every website is not only functional but truly captivating. Be sure to check out her recent projects on the main page to see her dedication to turning ideas into reality. Beyond her technical expertise, she brings a friendly and approachable attitude to every project, making collaboration seamless and enjoyable.

Her vision for Web Revive Studio is rooted in helping businesses grow and thrive. Through thoughtful design and unwavering commitment, she strives to empower clients by delivering high-ranking websites that expand their reach and drive success. Her enthusiasm for each project, combined with a "don't stop until it’s done" mindset, ensures every client gets timely and excellent results. Outside of coding, she’s a skilled ballroom and Latin dancer and a knowledgeable dog behaviorist/trainer, bringing creativity, discipline, and compassion to every aspect of her work and life.
